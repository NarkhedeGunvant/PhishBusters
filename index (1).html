How to Identify Phishing

Common signs of phishing websites

  • Suspicious URLs

    Look for misspellings or extra characters in domain names

  • Poor Design

    Legitimate sites usually have professional design and no spelling errors

  • Urgent Requests

    Be wary of sites creating urgency or threatening consequences

  • Unsecured Connection

    Check for HTTPS and a padlock icon in the address bar

  • Unusual Requests

    Legitimate sites won't ask for sensitive information via email

Email Phishing Indicators

Common signs of phishing emails

  • Mismatched Sender

    Email address doesn't match the claimed organization

  • Urgent Language

    Messages creating urgency or threatening negative consequences

  • Suspicious Links

    Hover over links to see if they lead to unexpected destinations

  • Requests for Information

    Legitimate organizations rarely request sensitive information via email

  • Grammar & Spelling

    Professional organizations typically have proper grammar and spelling
